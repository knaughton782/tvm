Basic Principles of Algebra

  • Cancel out variables or numbers by performing the same action to both sides of the equal side.
  • Find the reciprocal of a number by converting the number to a fraction, then switching the numerator and denominator. A number multiplied by its reciprocal equals 1. Reciprocals are useful for canceling numbers and exponents.

Order of Operations

You'll need to know the order of operations to understand the math that follows. Remember PEMDAS:

  • Parenthesis
  • Exponents
  • Multiplication and Division (from left to right)
  • Addition and Subtraction (from left to right)
