Introduction

Introduction

This is the first of four tutorials on the Time Value of Money. In this first part, we'll start wtih the basic equation and variables. Subsequent tutorials will add complexity by introducing compound interest, payments, and annuities.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this tutorial, you will:

  • Understand the relationship between variables in the TVM equation
  • Memorize and recall the general form of the TVM equation
  • Identify the TVM variables within a given word problem
  • Perform mathematical operations to solve for the unknown variable in the TVM equation, assuming annual interest

Prereqs

To achieve the objectives in this tutorial, you will need:

  • Basic algebra skills, including Order of Operations (remember the acronym PEMDAS) and FOIL

TVM may require you to dust off some math skills that you haven’t used in a while. Contact your instructor if you need additional help.

Requirements

To complete this tutorial, you will need:

  • Paper
  • A pencil or pen
  • A calculator (optional: a financial calculator

Once you have everything you need, let’s get started!
